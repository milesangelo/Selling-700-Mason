# Phase B Quick Wins - Low/No Cost Items

Tasks you can start now while Phase A sales generate funds for larger repairs.

---

## Tier 1: Zero Cost Assessment (Do First)
**Time Investment: ~2 hours total**

### 1. Count Missing Receptacle Covers
- [ ] Walk entire house with notepad
- [ ] Document location and type needed (standard toggle, decorator, GFCI, etc.)
- [ ] Take photos of each location
- [ ] Create shopping list with quantities
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 30 min | **Impact:** Know exact budget need ($1-3 per cover)

### 2. Locate and Count Open Junction Boxes
- [ ] Walk unfinished basement areas
- [ ] Document each open box location
- [ ] Note box size and type for correct covers
- [ ] Take photos for reference
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 15 min | **Impact:** Know exact budget need (~$2-5 per cover)

### 3. Assess Office Closet Floor Damage
- [ ] Inspect damage size and cause
- [ ] Check if joists are accessible/sound
- [ ] Measure area needing repair
- [ ] Determine repair method (patch vs. section replacement)
- [ ] Create materials list
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 15 min | **Impact:** Know exact materials needed (~$30-50 total)

---

## Tier 2: Research & Contractor Prep (Do Second)
**Time Investment: ~3 hours total**

### 4. Research HVAC Contractors (Mini-Split Repair)
- [ ] Google search: "HVAC mini-split repair Kennett Square PA"
- [ ] Check Google reviews and ratings (look for 4+ stars)
- [ ] Create list of 5-7 candidates with phone numbers
- [ ] Note which ones specialize in mini-splits or heat pumps
- [ ] Check if they service your mini-split brand
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 45 min | **Deliverable:** Ready-to-call list

### 5. Research Roofing Contractors
- [ ] Google search: "roofing contractors Kennett Square PA"
- [ ] Check Google reviews, BBB ratings, Angi/HomeAdvisor
- [ ] Create list of 5-7 candidates with phone numbers
- [ ] Note if they offer free estimates
- [ ] Check if licensed/insured (should be stated on website)
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 45 min | **Deliverable:** Ready-to-call list

### 6. Research Stucco Repair Contractors
- [ ] Google search: "stucco repair Kennett Square PA" or "masonry contractors"
- [ ] Check reviews and project photos
- [ ] Create list of 3-5 candidates with phone numbers
- [ ] Note specialty (some do general masonry, some focus on stucco)
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 30 min | **Deliverable:** Ready-to-call list

### 7. Research Licensed Electricians
- [ ] Google search: "licensed electrician Kennett Square PA"
- [ ] Verify license status (PA requires licensing)
- [ ] Create list of 3-5 candidates with phone numbers
- [ ] Note if they do residential panel work
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 30 min | **Deliverable:** Ready-to-call list

### 8. Create Contractor Contact Sheet
- [ ] Consolidate all contractor research into one document
- [ ] Format: Trade | Business Name | Phone | Website | Notes
- [ ] Add columns for: Called (Y/N) | Quote Received | Amount
- **Cost:** $0 | **Time:** 15 min | **Deliverable:** Single tracking document

## Tier 3: Low-Cost DIY Fixes (Do Third)
**Time Investment: ~4-6 hours | Cost: ~$80-120 total**

### 9. Purchase & Install Receptacle Covers
- [ ] Buy covers based on assessment (Home Depot/Lowe's)
- [ ] Install all missing covers
- **Cost:** ~$20-40 | **Time:** 1-2 hours | **Impact:** Immediate inspection fix

### 10. Purchase & Install Junction Box Covers
- [ ] Buy appropriate covers for basement boxes
- [ ] Install covers on all accessible boxes
- [ ] Verify wire connections are secure (if visible)
- **Cost:** ~$15-30 | **Time:** 1 hour | **Impact:** Immediate inspection fix

### 11. Patch Office Closet Floor
- [ ] Purchase materials (plywood, screws, wood filler)
- [ ] Cut and fit patch to damaged area
- [ ] Secure to joists properly
- [ ] Sand and finish (if visible)
- **Cost:** ~$30-50 | **Time:** 2-3 hours | **Impact:** Immediate inspection fix

---

## Recommended Sequence

### Week 1: Assessment & Research (Phase A ongoing)
1. Count receptacle covers (30 min)
2. Count junction boxes (15 min)
3. Assess closet floor (15 min)
4. Research all contractors (3 hours)
5. Create contractor tracking sheet (15 min)

**Result:** Complete list of what's needed and who to call when funds arrive

### Week 2-3: Low-Cost Fixes (as Phase A funds trickle in)
6. Buy and install receptacle covers (~$30)
7. Buy and install junction box covers (~$20)
8. Buy materials and patch closet floor (~$40)

**Result:** 3 inspection items completely resolved for ~$90 total

### Week 3-4: Schedule Quote Appointments (as Phase A nears completion)
9. Call HVAC contractors for mini-split quotes (3 minimum)
10. Call roofing contractors for assessments (3 minimum)
11. Call electricians for panel evaluation (2-3)
12. Call stucco contractors for assessment (2-3)

**Result:** All quotes in hand, ready to prioritize repairs when Phase A funds are available
